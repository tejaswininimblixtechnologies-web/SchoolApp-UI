## Sections Data Requirements
1. **Dashboard**: Student Name, Class & Section, Roll Number, Overall Attendance %, Upcoming Class/Exam, Pending Assignments count, Fee Due status, Recent Notifications (last 3)
2. **Attendance**: Total Working Days, Days Present, Days Absent, Attendance Percentage, Monthly Attendance view, Date-wise status
3. **Marks/Results**: Exam Name, Subject-wise Marks, Total Marks, Percentage, Grade, Result Status, Teacher Remarks
4. **Timetable**: Day-wise schedule, Subject Name, Time slot, Teacher Name, Classroom/Online link, Break timings
5. **Assignments**: Assignment Title, Subject, Assigned Date, Due Date, Submission Status, Upload Assignment button, Teacher Feedback
6. **Fee & Payments**: Total Fee, Paid Amount, Pending Amount, Due Date, Payment Status, Payment History, Download Receipt button
7. **Notifications**: Message Title, Description, Date & Time, Category, Read/Unread status
8. **Profile**: Student Name, Roll Number, Class & Section, Date of Birth, Parent Name & Contact, Email Address, Address, Profile Picture, Update Profile button
